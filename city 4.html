Proximity to Family and Friends

Scottsdale has a reputation as a travel destination, but it’s also a city beloved by residents. If you’re thinking about moving to the desert Southwest, Scottsdale deserves consideration. A move to Scottsdale promises abundant amenities, from vibrant outdoor activities and sports to a thriving downtown and waterfront. The city of about 220,000 is known for excellent quality of life, as well as tourist appeal (the city has the fourth-most AAA Four Diamond hotels in the U.S.).
